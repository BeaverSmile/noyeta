About

Noyeta

Find Pilates/Yoga/Relief videos on YouTube distraction-free with variety.

Problem Origin

I am a huge yoga fan, and there are so many great yoga videos on YouTube. There are 3 problems I faced when looking for videos:

  1. The same results are at the top every time. This means I either do the same routines every time, or I have to dig deep for a fresh video.
  2. YouTube can be distracting. When I load it up and it shows me the feed of recommended videos, it's easy to get side tracked.
  3. Which video is the "one"? With so many choices, it's easy to get overwhelmed with finding the "best" one for the moment.

Solution

Noyeta!

Noyeta is a collection of Pilates/Yoga/Relief YouTube videos organized by length of time or what they target. It randomly picks a video from a pool based on your desired routine. No more hunting down videos! You won't get distracted either since the only thing between you and your ideal video is a few button clicks.
